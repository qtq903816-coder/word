# Data Sources

WorkWord uses public/open data sources to build the vocabulary bank.

- New General Service List, TOEIC Service List, and Business Service List from the NGSL project: CC BY-SA 4.0.
- ECDICT mini by skywind3000: MIT license.

The import script downloads source CSV files, filters them for TOEIC, workplace, business, and technology learning, then generates `src/wordBank.js`.

The generated app examples are template sentences created for this project, not copied from commercial vocabulary sites.
